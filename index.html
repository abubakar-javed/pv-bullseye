Privacy Policy for Bullseye

Last Updated: March 19, 2024

Welcome to Bullseye. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you use our mobile application and tell you about your privacy rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following types of information:

  • Personal identification information (Name, email address, phone number)
  • Financial information (Investment preferences, portfolio details)
  • Profile information (Profile picture, user preferences)
  • Device information (Device type, operating system)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our investment platform services
  • Connect investors with financial advisors
  • Process and manage investment transactions
  • Send you important updates about your investments
  • Improve our services and user experience

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. This includes:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security assessments
  • Secure data storage and transmission
  • Access controls and authentication

4. Data Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Financial advisors (with your consent)
  • Service providers who assist in our operations
  • Legal authorities when required by law

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Withdraw consent

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@bullseye.com

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
